About AgriVision

AgriVision is an AI-powered platform designed to empower farmers with smart agricultural insights. Our goal is to make farming more productive, sustainable, and profitable by providing:

  • Accurate crop recommendations based on soil and weather.
  • Smart fertilizer suggestions to optimize yield.
  • Weekly weather forecasts for better planning.
  • AI-powered plant disease detection to identify and treat crop diseases.
  • Upcoming features like crop pricing insights and government scheme updates.

We believe in combining technology with agriculture to help farmers make informed decisions and secure their livelihoods.
